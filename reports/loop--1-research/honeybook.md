# HoneyBook — Competitor Analysis

## Overview
HoneyBook is a clientflow management platform for service-based creative small businesses — photographers, wedding planners, designers, coaches, and consultants. Covers lead capture, scheduling, contracts, invoicing, payments, automations, and a branded client portal.

## Features
- Lead capture with contact forms and AI lead-scoring alerts
- Interactive Smart Files (proposals, contracts, invoices in one link)
- Scheduling with calendar sync and automated reminders
- Invoicing with payment plans, deposits, and ACH/card processing
- E-signatures and contract management
- AI Notetaker for meetings, AI email assistant, daily action plans
- Branded client portal with custom domain
- Automations for client workflows
- QuickBooks, Calendly, Zoom, Zapier, Google Calendar integrations
- Mobile app for iOS and Android

## UX
HoneyBook is widely regarded as the most polished client experience platform in the creative services category. The Smart Files feature — combining proposals, contracts, and invoicing into one seamless client-facing document — is consistently cited as the best-in-class experience. 60% daily active usage rate (roughly 2x typical CRM software).

## Pricing
- Starter: $29/mo annual ($36/mo) — unlimited clients/projects, basic invoicing, proposals, contracts
- Essentials: $49/mo annual ($59/mo) — adds automation, AI features, 2 team members, branded portal
- Premium: $109/mo annual ($129/mo) — unlimited team members, priority support, advanced reporting
- Card processing: 2.9% + $0.25 (cardholder-entered), 3.4% + $0.09 (card-on-file/Amex/Discover)
- ACH: 1.5% flat; instant deposit: +1% fee
- US and Canada only — no international support

## Strengths
- Best-in-class client portal and Smart Files experience
- 4.7/5 on Capterra (679+ reviews), 4.4/5 on G2 (188+ reviews)
- Flat-rate pricing (not per-user) — good value for growing teams
- AI features bundled on all plans (Notetaker, email assistant, lead scoring)
- Customer support rated 4.7/5 across 679 reviews
- 60-day money-back guarantee on first paid month

## Weaknesses
- February 2025 price hike: Starter went from $19 to $36 (89% jump) — still driving negative reviews
- 2-3 business day card payout times (Square offers next-day)
- Card-on-file rate jumps to 3.4% vs advertised 2.9%
- US and Canada only — no international availability
- Mobile app limited vs desktop for advanced features
- 3.5/5 on Trustpilot (570 reviews) — billing complaints concentrate here
- BBB customer reviews average 1/5 — funds-hold disputes during KYC

## User Complaints
- 89% price hike on Starter plan (Feb 2025) is the #1 complaint on Trustpilot and Reddit
- Slow payment deposits (2-3 days for card, 7-8 days for ACH)
- Hidden card-on-file surcharge (3.4% + $0.09 vs advertised 2.9% + $0.25)
- Email deliverability issues reported by some users
- New merchant accounts flagged during KYC verification with funds held
- No international support (US/Canada only)

## Review Summary
- Capterra: 4.7/5 (683 reviews) — ease of use 4.6, support 4.7
- G2: 4.4/5 (188 reviews) — 75% are 5-star
- Trustpilot: 3.5/5 (570 reviews) — 58% 5-star, 10% 1-star
- BBB: A+ business rating, 1.0/5 customer review (10 reviews)
- PCMag: "A pricey CRM that serves established SMBs well"

## Overall Assessment
HoneyBook is the gold standard for client experience in creative services. Post-2025 price hike, it's best for established businesses booking structured engagements. Solo Suite's opportunity: offer comparable client experience at a more accessible price point, with international support and faster payouts.
