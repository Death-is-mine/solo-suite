# AND.CO (Fiverr Workspace) — Competitor Analysis

## Overview
AND.CO was a popular freelance business management platform acquired by Fiverr and rebranded as Fiverr Workspace. It offers contracts, invoicing, time tracking, expense management, and proposals in a clean interface. The tool is now integrated with Fiverr's marketplace ecosystem.

## Features
- Invoicing with customizable templates, recurring billing, and payment reminders
- Contract builder with legally vetted templates and e-signatures
- Time tracking with one-touch timer and "double dip" (track two timers simultaneously)
- Expense tracking with receipt capture (email or photo)
- Proposals and client quotes
- Client portal with dedicated PayMe page
- Project management with task and deadline tracking
- Payment processing: PayPal, credit card, ACH transfers
- Multi-currency support
- Financial reporting and tax management
- Bank account connections (up to 6 on Pro)
- Mobile app (iOS and Android)
- Integrations: Stripe, PayPal, Slack, QuickBooks, Zapier, Calendly

## UX
Fiverr Workspace maintains AND.CO's clean, approachable interface. Users consistently praise its intuitive design, especially for those new to freelance management software. It's described as "a breath of fresh air" compared to more complex platforms. The simplicity is both a strength and a limitation — easy to start but limited for advanced needs.

## Pricing
- Free tier: Basic invoicing and contracts with Fiverr branding, 1 client
- Pro (Unlimited): $18/mo (annual) or $24/mo — unlimited clients, custom branding, editable contracts, 6 bank connections, priority support
- 30-day money-back guarantee
- Free trial available

## Strengths
- Clean, intuitive interface — praised by users as "a breath of fresh air"
- Free tier available for getting started
- Legally vetted contract templates
- Fiverr marketplace integration for Fiverr freelancers
- Affordable Pro plan at $18/mo
- Multi-currency support for international freelancers

## Weaknesses
- Limited payment integrations (Stripe and PayPal only) — frustrating for international clients
- No customizable proposal templates — users note this as a gap
- Stripe integration can be occasionally unreliable
- Customer service inconsistent — helpful sometimes, slow other times
- Under Fiverr ownership — product direction may prioritize Fiverr marketplace users
- Lacks the depth of features in Bonsai or HoneyBook
- Smaller user community than category leaders

## User Complaints
- Limited payment options (only Stripe and PayPal) — loses business from clients who prefer alternatives
- Proposal capabilities are weak compared to dedicated tools
- Occasional Stripe integration issues causing payment delays
- Customer support quality varies significantly
- Fiverr acquisition created uncertainty about long-term product direction
- Features feel plateaued — less active development than competitors

## Review Summary
- SelectHub: 95% user satisfaction rating (373 reviews)
- Tekpon: 4.0/5 — praised for ease of use, criticized for limited features
- GetApp: ~4.3/5 — valued for time tracking and invoicing simplicity
- Praised for: clean design, time tracking, ease of setup
- Criticized for: limited payment options, weak proposals, support inconsistency
- 95% satisfaction rating on SelectHub across 373 reviews

## Overall Assessment
AND.CO/Fiverr Workspace is a solid entry-level freelance platform with a clean UX, but it has plateaued in features. Solo Suite's opportunity: offer a similarly clean interface with broader payment options, stronger proposals, and more active development — without the marketplace-dependency concerns.
