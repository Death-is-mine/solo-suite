# Dubsado — Competitor Analysis

## Overview
Dubsado is a business management platform for creative service businesses, offering lead capture, contracts, invoicing, scheduling, and powerful workflow automation (Flows). Available globally. Major competitor to HoneyBook in the creative space.

## Features
- Lead capture forms (1 on Starter, unlimited on Premier)
- Proposals, contracts, invoices with e-signatures
- Client portals with branded forms
- Automated workflows (Flows) with triggers, delays, task assignments
- Scheduling with recurring availability and booking
- Time tracking and expense management
- Bookkeeping integration (QuickBooks, Xero) — Premier only
- Zapier integration — Premier only
- Dubsado Payments (Stripe-powered): 2.9% + $0.30 US cards, 0.8% ACH capped at $5
- Multi-brand support ($10/mo per additional brand)
- Mobile app
- Global availability (multi-currency card payments)

## UX
Dubsado offers deep customization and powerful automation, but has a steep learning curve. Capterra reviewers give it 3.6/5 for ease of use. The Flows builder is powerful but typically takes about a week to configure properly. Interface rewards users who read documentation. Dubsado 3.0 (Nov 2025) added a unified inbox, Kanban/table views, and a rebuilt scheduler.

## Pricing
- Starter: $335/yr ($35/mo) — forms (1 active), contracts, invoicing, client portals, 3 users
- Premier: $525/yr ($55/mo) — adds unlimited forms, scheduling, Flows automations, proposals, Zapier, bookkeeping sync
- Additional users: $25/mo (4-10), $45/mo (11-20), $60/mo (21-30)
- Additional brands: $10/mo each
- Dubsado 3.0 launched Dec 2025 with price reset (Starter went $20→$35, Premier $40→$55)
- Existing users grandfathered at old rates

## Strengths
- Deepest workflow automation in the creative CRM category (Flows builder)
- Global availability (unlike HoneyBook's US/Canada limitation)
- ACH fee capped at $5 — significantly cheaper than HoneyBook's 1.5% uncapped
- White-label branding on forms, proposals, and client portal
- Dubsado 3.0 modernized the interface significantly
- Captures ~38% market share in photographer communities (vs HoneyBook's 19%)
- 21-day free trial with full Premier access, no credit card

## Weaknesses
- Dec 2025 price hike: Starter +75%, Premier +38%
- Steep learning curve — not plug-and-play
- Starter plan is severely limited (no workflows, scheduling, Zapier)
- Workflow limitations: no true conditional logic inside Flows
- No native Gantt charts or advanced project management
- Team members can't have separate calendars or email addresses
- Performance issues reported on G2 (bugginess, slow page loads)
- Mobile app is basic

## User Complaints
- Starter plan described as "a trap" — $335/yr for what free tools can do
- Workflow builder has a steep learning curve; takes weeks to master
- No conditional logic in workflows; can't send to alternate contacts
- Performance issues and bugginess flagged in G2 reviews
- Dec 2025 price hike hit new users hard (75% increase on Starter)
- Mobile app is limited and basic

## Review Summary
- G2: 4.3/5 (74+ reviews) — implementation under 1 month, ROI within 4 months
- Capterra: ~4.2/5 (61+ reviews) — valued for workflow automation and branding
- GetApp: 4.2/5 — praised for streamlining workflow and payment automation
- Prospeo: "the best client management CRM for creatives who want deep automation"
- Criticized for: learning curve, Starter plan limitations, performance issues

## Overall Assessment
Dubsado is the deepest automation platform in the creative CRM space, but requires serious setup investment. Solo Suite's opportunity: offer powerful automation with a gentler learning curve and more accessible pricing, plus modern project management features Dubsado lacks.
